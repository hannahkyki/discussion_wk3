Simple Homemade Lemonade

Photo by Ang√©lica Echeverry on Unsplash

Ingredients

  • Lemon Juice - 2 tablespoons
  • Sugar - 1/4 cup
  • Water - 4-6 cups

Instructions

  1. In a large pitcher, combine lemon juice, sugar and water. Stir until the sugar has dissolved.
  2. Taste it! Add water or sugar to taste.
  3. Add ice to chill, and store in the fridge.
  4. You can add whatever you'd like to this simple recipe! Some suggestions: raspberries, mint leaves, strawberries. You can also substitute lemons for limes and add a bit more sugar.
